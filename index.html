Your waiters and kitchen staff can view Instalacarte orders in real-time through a dedicated staff portal or a PWA (Progressive Web App) designed for order management. Each staff member can log in on their respective devices (tablets, smartphones) to see new orders, mark them as prepared, and manage order statuses. Notifications can be configured to alert staff about new incoming orders.

Instalacarte operates primarily as a web-based application and a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means it doesn't require a traditional app store listing. Customers access your menu directly by scanning a QR code, and staff manage orders through a web portal. This approach avoids app store download barriers and ensures instant updates.
